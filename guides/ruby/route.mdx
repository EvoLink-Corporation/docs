### Scheduling

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Discord">

    Use the `/route`command within the Ruby Assistant Bot.

  <Tab title="Roblox">
    <Warning>
      This uses software not made by RBS. As a staff member, you comply to follow their (Series - Sailboat Games LLC) Terms of Service and Privacy Policy at all times to not face legal action.
    </Warning>
    1. Navigate to https://workspaces.series.hr/ and log-in with your RBLX account. You may be asked to join the company in the bottom section of this page if you haven't already.
    2. Find the "Sessions" tab in the left sidebar.
    3. Click Create Session and add the following information
       - Title: [TIME] [AM/PM] Route - [RBLX USERNAME] - EX: 8 PM Route - unxnownyt. All times should be in Eastern Time.
       - Host: YOU, Co-Host: PICK, Supervisor: PICK or Not Appicable
       - Date: Day of your Route; Time: Time of your route.
       - Individual Session; Duration 1 Hour.
    4. Hit Create Session.

    > _A Manager or Department Director may remove you from a session at any time, shape or form and give it to somebody else._

    Roblox Group:\
    We do not announce sessions on our RBLX Group unless it is a special event or a Field Trip due to not many people using our RBLX Group for announcements about these routes. _We may change this in the future._
  </Tab>
